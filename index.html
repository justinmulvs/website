About Me

I've made a career out of supporting world-class entrepreneurs and leaders. I started working at my family's business at the age of 12. My first job out of college was employee #3 at a fast-growing tech startup. After that, I never joined a company later than employee #10. I've led the product and data side of an acquisition, run data for a $9M fundraise, and supported C-level leaders in every department of the modern tech org.

Those supporting roles led me to my passion for coaching and the decision to become an entrepreneur.

As a coach, I've worked with clients in NYC, Nashville, and Boston. I've helped solopreneurs grow their income by 50%. I've supported founders through fundraises and acquisitions. And I've helped burgeoning leaders grow into their full potential.
