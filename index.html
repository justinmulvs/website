As a leader, do you recognize any of these challenges?

  • You struggle to stop working “in” your business and start working “on” it.
  • You feel like your team (including your senior leaders) isn't as effective as it could be. You’re aware that you somehow get in the way, but are unclear how.
  • You get overwhelmed and struggle to identify what's most important. You find yourself procrastinating and avoiding. This drives a sense of impostor syndrome—a belief that you’re not “really” a leader, and eventually you'll be "found out".
  • You feel burned out and unfulfilled. Something is off, but you're not sure what.
  • You feel a pervasive sense of loneliness. You believe that no one around you “gets it”.
  • You struggle with fear, uncertainty, and doubt. You're unsure if it's normal, what to do with it, or how to process it.
  • You struggle to find time to work on yourself.
  • You desire a partner to give you feedback and help optimize your leadership. You're high performing, but know you can be better.

Underneath it all you know that something needs to change. You may even want to change. But the change isn’t happening, and if it is, it’s not in the way that you’d like.

You're stuck. And that’s where I come in.

I help ambitious, big-hearted leaders at pivotal moments in their careers.

I work from the belief that your outer results—the team that's underperforming, the conversations you're avoiding, the anxiety that won't go away—are direct reflections of your inner world. I coach leaders on the "inner game" of leadership, using their results as doorways to uncover the patterns driving them, evolve how they navigate emotions under pressure, and shift how they see themselves and others.

The leaders I work with report achieving better results while experiencing more ease and enjoyment in their work. They trust themselves more deeply, communicate more directly and effectively, and build more deep and satisfying relationships. In short: they succeed AND they thrive.

Client Testimonials

About Me

Hi, I'm Justin. I'm a Conscious Leadership and Executive Coach, a Facilitator for leadership teams, and a passionate practitioner of what I preach. Since 2020, I've had the pleasure of coaching 100+ executives and leadership teams across the YC, NYC, YPO, and Hampton ecosystems. I love what I do.

I help founders, investors, and executives achieve sustainable excellence—performing at their best and building exceptional teams without burning out.

I specialize in supporting leaders at pivotal moments in their journeys, when they're called to rise to the occasion of something greater than where they've been. The leaders I work best with are ambitious, big-hearted, self-inquisitive, and highly invested in their personal development. Many are what I call "owner-parents"—leaders with exceptional ownership in their careers who are also growing families, at peak-building phases both personally and professionally. They want to succeed in their professional life and thrive in their personal life, and stubbornly refuse to compromise on either front.

Before coaching, I spent nearly a decade in leadership roles at venture-backed technology startups. I was COO at Ravti (led product, analytics, and cross-functional ops through an acquisition), Business Intelligence Manager at Spacious (led data for a $9M fundraise), and employee #3 at Crystal. Thanks to these experiences, I bring real, in-the-trenches operational and leadership experience to my coaching.

I am an avid believer and practitioner of conscious leadership. I'm a certified 15 Commitments of Conscious Leadership coach and devotedly practice in my day-to-day life. I've also trained in somatics, parts work, developmental coaching, and am certified as an Enneagram practitioner. I draw from all of these modalities to support my clients to better understand themselves and become exceptional leaders.

I live in Beacon, NY with my wife and son. I am deeply passionate about my family. Becoming a father has given me more purpose than I ever could have imagined.

If you’re interested and want to be supported, held, and lovingly challenged, all in the name of your personal growth, please reach out. It would be a delight to connect with you.
