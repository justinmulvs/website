  • What this is

    I like to read a lot of books, mostly nonfiction. I take notes while I read, and have polished them up for you to read below. They're organized by categories. If I put a book here, it's a personal endorsement.


  • How to use this

    I like to think about it in two ways:
    1. Gauge interest in buying: We all like to check things out before buying them. You can take your chance with reviews, but know that these are better: They're a damn sampler platter. Want to know if you're interested in a book? Take a peak at what's inside, and decide from there.

    2. Reference Guides: I wrote these so I could better remember the information and refer back to them when I couldn't. I encourage you to do the same! Read them over, learn what you can. But when you can't remember that one important thing- they'll still be here! I'd consider bookmarking the page, if you like them enough.
    Reach out if you have any questions/want to discuss further! If you can't tell, I'm a book nerd who loves to discuss.


  • Why?

    I make them anyway, I enjoy teaching, and knowledge is power.
