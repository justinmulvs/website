Retreats & Offsites

One candid, curated offsite is all that it takes to transform your team.

I partner with forward-thinking organizations to create custom-tailored, transformational experiences for their next offsite. I design my programming uniquely for your organization’s needs and include a mix of immersive activities, tailored workshops, and impactful discussions. Everything is designed to nurture connection, candor, openness, and strategic thinking.

Prior experiences have included programming with proven exercises designed to:

  • Identify collective gaps, dysfunctions, and key opportunity areas
  • See how your work histories influence how you show up and relate to each other today
  • Uncover the core patterns and anti-patterns that impact how you work together
  • Enable you to safely speak the candid truths and feedback that will actually make a difference for your ability to collaborate and execute
  • Create a deep and long-lasting sense of appreciation for each other as a team
  • Determine your organization’s mission, vision, values and how to implement them
  • Empower a deep sense of connection for a newly forming company and team
  • Integrate all of this into more effective execution in your day-to-day at work

Teams that work with me report a greater understanding of how to effectively work together, a feeling of deeper connection, and an increase in effectiveness, both as individuals and as a team.
